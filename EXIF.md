```html
<h2>竖拍的图片</h2>
<div class="item-pic"><img src="./images/IMG_0387.JPG"></div>
<h2>横拍的图片</h2>
<div class="item-pic"><img src="./images/IMG_0386.JPG"></div>
```

![](./images/IMG_0392.PNG)
#####Android里面竖拍的图片的显示的方向不对
